Meet the Artists

Banksy

Banksy is the pseudonym of an anonymous British street artist, political activist, and film director. Active since the 1990s, his identity remains officially unconfirmed, a mystery that has become integral to his global appeal.

“Banksy art on Brick Lane” - 2004
